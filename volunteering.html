For the last 20+ years we've worked with local shelters to save over 10,000 cats.

In 1996, Partners for Pets was nothing more than a few people’s passion for helping animals. Not much has changed since then. We are a group of animal lovers dedicated to finding the purrfect fit for every cat and kitten in our care. For every one we place, we are back at the shelter rescuing another.

When a kitty joins our program, one of the first things we do is schedule a check-up with a veterinarian where they are sterilized, micro-chipped, vaccinated, and tested for major diseases. While they recover, they stay in the care of a foster home until they are ready to move into our adoption center and find their forever home. As a part of the fostering process, we have the unique opportunity to get to know the kitties in a home environment so we can assess their individual needs and help them find the best possible home. If for any reason their adopted home does not work out we welcome them back into our program (even years later).
