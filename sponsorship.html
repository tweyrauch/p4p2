A small monthly donation can help us care for all the cats that need our help!

When you can’t adopt another pet because your home is already full. When you live in an apartment and can’t have pets. When you already have a pet that doesn’t play well with others. When you love what we are doing and still want to help. Sponsorship is designed for you.

Because we are a no-kill rescue, we often keep our rescues for a long time. It is not uncommon for a cat to spend months, if not years, in our care. Whether a rescue is shy and needs time to become socialized, or just takes a little extra time to find their purrfect fit, our program gives them the chance to do so. However, the costs for caring for these cats goes up the longer they stay in our program. They require more food, more litter, and often more vaccinations and vet visits. Sponsorship funds provide a helps to pay for these extra costs, so that our adoption fees can go towards the rescue of additional cats instead.

The first step is signing up!

You can sign up to sponsor a Partners for Pets rescue either online or by mail. Once you are signed up, you will receive receive a “Welcome” email from our sponsorship coordinator who will also help you choose the cat you want to sponsor. Or, if you are signing up by mail and already know what cat(s) you would like to sponsor you can include their name on the sponsorship application. The pets that need sponsorship are adults or those that are shy or have special needs. These are the ones that take longer to find homes, so their financial needs are higher.

Once your sponsorship is all set up we'll send you quarterly updates on the cat you've selected. When the great day comes and your sponsor cat is adopted you get the news and the opportunity to select a new rescue to help. At the end of the year you’ll get a letter confirming the amount of your donations for tax purposes. Most importantly, you’ll know that you’re helping the most needy of our pets get a chance to find their forever home!
Sign Up Online

Our online sponsorship is designed to make things easier for you. By signing up online, your sponsorship donation will be made automatically every month through your PayPal account. You can cancel these payments at any time through your PayPal account (details below).

The donation for our online sponsorship is $16 per cat sponsored; the extra dollar makes up for the processing fees associated with the convenience of having an online option.

Sign up Today »

Sign Up by Mail

To join via mail, please print and mail in the sponsorship application with your first donation. We'll take care of the rest!

The recommended donation is $15 per month per cat sponsored if you sign up by mail. Some sponsors pay each month, some pay several months in advance.

Note: We can only accept cash or check for mailed sponsorships.

Download Sponsorship Application »

Canceling Your Online Sponsorship Payments

Online sponsorship subscriptions can be canceled at any time through your PayPal account.

How to cancel:

  1. Log in to your PayPal account
  2. Go to settings
  3. Select "Payments"
  4. Click "Manage Automatic Payments"
  5. Select "Partners for Pets" from the list (make sure you have "Show Active" selected")
  6. Click the "Cancel" button

See full instructions for canceling on your PayPal mobile app or from a computer »
